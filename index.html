Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose Pre-Cleaning over just Water Pressure?

Water pressure alone only removes surface dirt. Both our Gold and Diamond packages use specialized pre-cleaning solutions that kill moss and algae at the root. This ensures a deeper clean and prevents organic growth from returning just weeks later.

What is included in the Post-Cleaning treatment?

Included in our Diamond Restoration & Long-Term Protection, the post-treatment is a brightening agent that eliminates "tiger stripes" (uneven wash lines) and leaves a residual barrier on the surface. This protective layer can keep your surfaces clean for up to 2 years.

Is the high pressure safe for my property?

Because our Gold and Diamond packages use specialized solutions to do the heavy lifting, we can use a 'Soft Wash' technique. This lower pressure is much safer for your siding, roof, and delicate surfaces while still achieving superior results.
